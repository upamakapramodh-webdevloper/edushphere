Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions.

How do I enroll in a course?

Visit the Courses page, select your preferred course, and click the Enroll button to start learning.

Can I attempt quizzes multiple times?

Yes. Practice quizzes can be attempted multiple times, while graded quizzes depend on course settings.

How do I submit assignments?

Open the Assignments page, choose your assignment, upload your file, and click Submit.

How can I contact support?

Use the contact form above or email our support team. We usually respond within 24 hours.
