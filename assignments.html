Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload multiple files?

Yes. You can upload multiple files if the assignment allows multiple attachments.

Can I edit my submission?

Yes, until the submission deadline. After the deadline, editing is disabled automatically.

How do I know my upload is successful?

A confirmation message and submission receipt will be displayed immediately after successful upload.

Where can I view faculty feedback?

Feedback will appear in your Dashboard and Profile after evaluation is completed.
